About Me

"Design is not just what it looks like and
feels like. Design is how it works." -Steve Jobs

I'm a UX Designer and Visual Designer from Atlanta, GA

Being an artist by trade has allowed me to create cutting edge, one-of-a-kind designs. The ability to captivate a user at a single glance expands far beyond what the eyes can see. Building a connection is the core to understanding the user. Having worked as a nurse, I have a passion for human-centered solutions. By having the user at the center of my design process, unique combination of structures that are simple, clean, engaging, and intuitive come to life.

When I’m not sketching an idea or working on a project, you’ll find me painting or playing with my toddler.
